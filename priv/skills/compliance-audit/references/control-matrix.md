# Control Matrix

Use the session's active compliance profile to select the relevant section.

## Baseline

- secrets
- injection
- XSS

## Healthcare

- PHI encryption
- role-based access
- audit logging
- breach process

## Finance

- no raw card data
- tokenization
- immutable financial records
- segregation of duties

## Education

- student-record disclosure
- age / consent checks
- access logging

## HR

- candidate data segregation
- no protected-attribute scoring
- salary data access controls

## Legal

- privilege isolation
- encrypted documents
- retention schedule

## Marketing / Sales / Real Estate

- consent / unsubscribe
- CRM or lead PII protection
- fair-housing and tenant-data controls
