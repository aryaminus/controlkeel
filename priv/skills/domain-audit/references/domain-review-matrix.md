# Domain Review Matrix

## HR
- protected classes never influence scoring or filtering
- candidate data is segregated and access controlled

## Legal
- privileged materials are isolated
- retention and eDiscovery expectations are preserved

## Marketing
- opt-in and unsubscribe controls exist
- analytics payloads avoid contact PII

## Sales
- CRM contacts have export / delete handling
- quota and revenue changes are auditable

## Real Estate
- fair-housing violations are blocked
- tenant data and identifiers are protected and retained appropriately

## Government / Public Sector
- records retention and records-request holds are preserved
- benefits, licensing, and case scoring avoid protected or citizenship-adjacent shortcuts

## Insurance / Claims
- denial or underwriting logic avoids protected and medical-attribute shortcuts
- claims notes, diagnoses, and payouts stay out of broad application logs

## E-commerce / Retail
- card, checkout-token, and refund-sensitive data never land in logs
- refund and chargeback automation preserves review thresholds

## Logistics / Supply Chain
- chain-of-custody and manifest history are not silently deleted
- dispatch and hazmat overrides require explicit review

## Manufacturing / Quality
- QA holds and recall traces remain auditable
- safety interlocks and lockout procedures cannot be bypassed in automation

## Nonprofit / Grants
- donor payment data is isolated and not logged
- beneficiary and program records are not bulk-exported without review
